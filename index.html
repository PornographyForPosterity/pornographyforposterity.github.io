PFP

Ensuring Anthropological Preservation of Pornographic Media

PFP catalogs the performers, studios, and releases of commercial pornography — starting with the pre-digital era, whose record is disappearing fastest. It hosts no films or images, only the historical metadata: names, credits, dates, and sources. See About for the full mission, Methodology for how entries are built, or browse performers, studios, and tags to the right.
